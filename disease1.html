Here are the symptoms and treatments for the common paediatric medical conditions.





What to Do:

• Keep him hydrated with lots of fluids like water and low-sugar juices
• Keep him away from crowded places,
sick individuals and other children
• Teach your child to use a tissue to blow his nose, or to cover his mouth when sneezing

• Bring your child to the doctor if he does not get better after a few days,
or if he is also running a high fever
